Salsa

Although the trumpet drove me to new rabbit holes,it was the supporting cast in Mariachi that keened my focus toward other orchestras. In comes Salsa. In this genre a similar foundation was discovered with completely different sonics. A new love was born for music where several instruments both unify to create one beautiful sound and are given individual time to shine.

Hit play on both and watch Dada go!
